Provide Sponsorship

Denver Dev Day is free, open to the public, and packed with practical training and information on software development. We anticipate around 200 attendees from our local and national communities. Denver Dev Day will have several tracks with various professional speakers and respected, community leaders. It will be held on at the Microsoft Offices in the Denver Technology Center, a campus that is friends with the developer community.

The success of Denver Dev Day is, in part, due to our sponsors. For a cash or in-kind donation, your company or group can enjoy excellent exposure to a segment of your target audience. We take it seriously to give your organization the exposure you deserve in front of the best, local audience anyone could muster.

Denver Dev Day cash sponsorship is handled via PayPal. For more information, please contact Daren May, the Denver Dev Day sponsorship manager at sponsors@denverdevday.com Please note that Denver Dev Day is not a not-for-profit. As a result, we cannot provide your organization with a tax-related receipt. We can, however, provide a standard receipt and our gratitude.

Denver Dev Day is an event produced for the community by the community. Sponsorship does not entail any oversight or selection of topics that will be presented, except where expressly stated. We will not disclose any personal information about the attendees to sponsors, and sponsorship does not mean we will give up this information. We respect our attendee's privacy. Attendees may give personal information to sponsors at the sponsorâ€™s table at their own discretion.

We accept both cash and in-kind donations, in where the in-kind donations are goods or services that are required to run an event such as this. Examples of in-kind donations are items or services used for door prizes, food and beverages, etc… Promotional material, door prizes, and "trinkets" that will be included in the attendee bags are not considered in-kind donations.

Cash sponsorships are due within 14 days of commitment, and in-kind donations are due two weeks prior to the event. Payment can be made via Paypal or check. Items to be included in the attendee bags should be delivered two weeks before the event date to ensure they are included. Items will be limited to one promotional item (flyer, printout, software, etc…).
